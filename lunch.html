TINOLANG MANOK

    INGREDIENTS:
  • 1 tbsp cooking oil
  • 1 pc onion, quartered
  • 1 (2 - inch) ginger, sliced
  • 1/2 kg chicken, cut into serving pcs.
  • 1 tbsp patis
  • 1 cup evaporated Milk
  • 1 cup water
  • 120 g. sayote or ¼ pc. papaya, cut into wedges
  • 1 pc Knorr Chicken Cubes
  • 1/2 bunch dahon ng sili
    INSTRUCTIONS:
  1. Begin by bringing your pot nice and hot to medium high heat. With a little bit of oil, sauté the onion and half of the ginger before adding in the chicken. Cook them well for a minute and give these a sprinkle of fish sauce.
  2. Throw in the sayote and pour in the evaporated milk, water and the remaining half of the ginger. Now, you have to let this simmer for a minute before throwing in the Knorr Chicken Cubes.
  3. When the chicken and vegetables are cooked, finish the soup by topping it with dahon ng sili.
