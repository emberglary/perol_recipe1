MUMBAI-STYLE VEG SANDWICH

    INGREDIENTS:
  • 8 slices of bread (white or whole wheat)
  • 2 medium-sized potatoes, boiled, peeled, and thinly sliced
  • 1 medium-sized onion, thinly sliced
  • 1 medium-sized tomato, thinly sliced
  • 1 medium-sized cucumber, thinly sliced
  • 1 medium-sized beetroot, boiled, peeled, and thinly sliced (optional)
  • Butter or ghee for toasting
  • Green chutney (mint and coriander chutney)
  • Chaat masala or sandwich masala
  • Salt to taste
    Green Chutney Ingredients:
  • 1 cup fresh coriander leaves
  • 1/2 cup fresh mint leaves
  • 2-3 green chilies (adjust according to taste)
  • 1-inch piece of ginger
  • 2-3 cloves of garlic
  • 1 teaspoon roasted cumin powder
  • 1 teaspoon chaat masala
  • Salt to taste
  • 1-2 tablespoons lemon juice
  • Water as needed
    INSTRUCTIONS:
    Green Chutney:
  1. In a blender, add coriander leaves, mint leaves, green chilies, ginger, garlic, roasted cumin powder, chaat masala, salt, and lemon juice.
  2. Blend to a smooth paste, adding water as needed to get the desired consistency.
  3. Transfer the chutney to a bowl and set aside.
    Sandwich Assembly:
  1. Take 4 slices of bread and spread a generous amount of green chutney on each slice.
  2. Place a layer of potato slices on two of the bread slices.
  3. On top of the potato slices, place onion slices, tomato slices, cucumber slices, and beetroot slices (if using).
  4. Sprinkle a little chaat masala or sandwich masala on top of the vegetables.
  5. Cover each sandwich with the remaining 4 slices of bread.
  6. Lightly butter the outer sides of each sandwich.
    Toasting:
  1. Heat a sandwich maker or a skillet over medium heat.
  2. Place the prepared sandwiches on the skillet or sandwich maker.
  3. Toast the sandwiches until they are golden brown and crisp on both sides.
  4. Remove from heat and cut each sandwich diagonally into two halves.
    SERVING:
  1. Serve Mumbai-style veg sandwiches hot with ketchup or additional green chutney on the side.
  2. Enjoy your delicious Mumbai-style veg sandwich!
