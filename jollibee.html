CHICKEN JOY

    INGREDIENTS:
  • Chicken: we are using drumsticks for this recipe. Feel free to use bone in thighs or chicken wings.
  • Flour: this recipe uses All Purpose Flour.
  • Cornstarch: cornstarch is often used in Asian fried chicken recipes. It's a must for extra crispy result.
  • Garlic Powder and Five Spice Powder: must haves! You can find Five Spice Powder at asian store.
  • Milk: regular milk works fine for this recipe. I am using 2% milk.
  • Egg: 1 large egg, this serves as the binder.
  • Salt and Black Pepper: to marinade chicken.
  • Oil: Vegetable oil or Canola oil works fine. For deep-frying chicken joy.
    INSTRUCTIONS:
  1. Season chicken with salt and black pepper to taste. Let it sit for 2-3 hours to marinate.
  2. To make the Flour mixture. In a bowl mix flour, cornstarch, garlic powder, five spice powder and salt. Stir until well combined. In a separate bowl, combine egg and milk.
  3. To assemble the chicken. Toss chicken to flour mixture until well coated. Transfer and dip chicken to egg mixture then back to flour mixture.
  4. Fry Chicken. In over medium heat deep pan or pot. Add 4 cups of oil. Deep fry chicken for 15 minutes turning each piece halfway, until chicken is golden brown and cooked through.
    5. Remove from the oil and drain excess oil. Let them rest for 10 minutes before serving.
