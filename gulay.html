CHICKEN SALAD

    INGREDIENTS:
  • 2 cups cooked chicken breast, shredded or diced
  • 1/2 cup mayonnaise
  • 1/4 cup celery, diced
  • 1/4 cup red onion, finely chopped
  • 1/4 cup grapes, halved
  • 1/4 cup almonds, sliced (optional)
  • 1 tablespoon fresh parsley, chopped
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Lettuce leaves (for serving)
  • Bread or crackers (optional, for serving)
  1. In a large mixing bowl, combine the cooked chicken, mayonnaise, celery, red onion, grapes, almonds (if using), and parsley. Stir until well combined.
  2. Season the mixture with salt and pepper to taste. Adjust the seasoning according to your preference.
  3. Cover the bowl and refrigerate the chicken salad for at least 30 minutes to allow the flavors to meld together.
  4. Once chilled, serve the chicken salad on a bed of lettuce leaves or as a sandwich filling between slices of bread. It can also be served with crackers as a snack or appetizer.

PERFECT ITALIAN SALAD

    INGREDIENTS:
  • 1 head of fresh, crisp lettuce (such as Romaine or butter lettuce), washed and torn into bite-sized pieces
  • 1 large tomato, sliced
  • 1/2 cucumber, sliced
  • 1/4 red onion, thinly sliced
  • 1/4 cup black olives, pitted
  • 1/4 cup fresh basil leaves, torn
  • 1/4 cup extra virgin olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons balsamic vinegar
  • Salt and freshly ground black pepper to taste
  • Optional: 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  1. Start by preparing the vegetables. Wash and chop the lettuce, tomato, cucumber, and red onion. Place them in a large salad bowl.
  2. Add the black olives and torn basil leaves to the bowl.
  3. In a small jar or bowl, whisk together the extra virgin olive oil and balsamic vinegar to make the dressing. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  4. Drizzle the dressing over the salad and toss gently to coat all the ingredients evenly.
  5. If desired, sprinkle grated Parmesan cheese over the top of the salad.
  6. Serve immediately as a side dish to your favorite Italian meal, or as a light and refreshing appetizer.
